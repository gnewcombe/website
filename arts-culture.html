Hyde Park Book Club

a mini-hub of culture, pleasantly positioned in one of Leeds' most prolific student areas Hyde Park, that offers everything between spoken word Poetry, live gigs from young artists on the rise, vegan nibbles too good to be true, comedy nights and recurring performances/events. During student season, you can expect diverse events every week from a "safe-space-first" staff who are very attentive to social injustices.
